Why you're here

We need you

You, dear students, are the chosen ones. You have the power to shape the future of South Africa's water and energy industries, and with it, the future of our entire nation. These industries are absolutely critical to our country's development, and they need bright young minds like yours to help drive them forward.

(Scroll right)

We need you

Firstly, let's talk about water. South Africa is a water-scarce country, and we're facing an ever-growing water crisis. Our water infrastructure is under enormous strain, and we're struggling to meet the needs of our growing population. But with the right expertise, we can find sustainable solutions to these challenges. As a water industry professional, you'll have the chance to work on projects that make a real difference in people's lives. Whether it's designing innovative new water treatment systems or finding ways to conserve water in agriculture, you'll be at the forefront of solving one of our country's most pressing problems.

We need you

Now, let's turn to energy. South Africa is a country with vast energy potential. We have abundant natural resources, including coal, oil, gas, and renewable sources like wind and solar power. But despite this, we're still struggling to meet the growing demand for energy in our homes, businesses, and industries. As an energy industry professional, you'll have the opportunity to work on projects that drive our country's economic growth and development. You could be part of the team that builds a new power plant, or helps to develop cutting-edge renewable energy technology. You'll be part of a vital industry that powers our lives and drives our country forward.

We need you

So why should you pursue a career in the South African water and energy industries? Because you have the chance to be part of something truly special. You have the power to make a real difference in people's lives, and to help shape the future of our entire country. You'll be joining a community of passionate, dedicated professionals who are committed to building a brighter, more sustainable future for all. So go ahead, be proud of the work you'll do, and take the first step towards an exciting and rewarding career in the South African water and energy industries!

About us

Who we are

The EWSETA, also known as the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority, is an organization in South Africa that's responsible for making sure people in the energy and water sectors have the skills they need to do their jobs well. Now, you might be thinking, "Who cares about energy and water? That sounds boring!" But hold on to your hats, because the EWSETA is actually a pretty cool organization. They're like the superheroes of the energy and water world, swooping in to make sure everyone has the training they need to save the day.

(Scroll right)

What we do

Imagine if you turned on your tap one day and nothing came out. Or if you flipped a switch and the lights didn't come on. That would be pretty terrible, right? Well, the folks at the EWSETA work hard to prevent that kind of thing from happening by making sure everyone who works in the energy and water sectors is properly trained. They do this by providing funding for training programs, developing qualifications and standards for different jobs, and making sure that companies are following those standards. So, if you've ever enjoyed a nice, warm shower or a cool glass of water on a hot day, you can thank the EWSETA for making sure the people who make those things possible are doing their jobs right.

Artisan Careers

Artisan careers offer a dynamic and exciting opportunity to make a positive impact on the country's economy and infrastructure.
